What We Do

Tenants deserve safe homes. Injured people deserve real representation.

We represent clients in Personal Injury and Habitability matters throughout Southern California, with a focus on unsafe rental housing, dangerous property conditions, falls, and injuries caused by negligent maintenance.

Habitability Law

California tenants have the right to safe, livable housing. The firm handles claims involving mold exposure, water intrusion, leaks, pests, failed repairs, and other unsafe rental conditions.

Mold and habitability cases may be handled on a contingency-fee basis: no attorney’s fees unless there is a recovery. Case costs are addressed separately and explained before representation begins.

Personal Injury

The firm also represents individuals injured because of dangerous property conditions, negligent maintenance, unsafe walkways, slip-and-fall incidents, trip-and-fall incidents, and other preventable hazards.

Personal injury matters are evaluated separately, and fee arrangements are discussed before representation begins.
