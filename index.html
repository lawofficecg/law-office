What We Do

Focused. Dedicated. On your side.

We represent tenants living in unsafe conditions and individuals injured by the negligence of others throughout Southern California.

Habitability Law

California law requires landlords to maintain safe, livable conditions. When they fail, mold, water damage, pest infestations, dangerous conditions, tenants have rights. We help them use those rights.
