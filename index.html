What We Do

Tenants deserve safe homes. Injured people deserve real representation.

We represent tenants living in uninhabitable conditions and individuals injured by someone else's negligence throughout Southern California.

Habitability Law

Mold, water damage, pests, broken heat — California law requires landlords to keep rental homes livable. When they don't, tenants have legal options. We take these cases on contingency: no fee unless you recover.

Personal Injury

When someone else's negligence causes you harm, you shouldn't bear the financial burden alone. We pursue compensation for medical bills, lost wages, pain and suffering, and more. Contact us to discuss fee arrangements.
