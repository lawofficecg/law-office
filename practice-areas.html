Practice Area

Habitability Law

Every tenant deserves a safe place to live. California law requires landlords to maintain rental properties free of mold, water damage, pest infestations, and other hazardous conditions. When they don't, they can be held legally responsible.

We represent tenants who are living in uninhabitable conditions, helping them get out of dangerous situations and recover compensation for the harm they've suffered.

If your landlord has ignored complaints about mold, leaks, or unsafe conditions, we want to hear from you.

Fee structure: Habitability and mold cases are handled on a contingency-fee basis. You pay no attorney fees unless your case resolves in your favor.
