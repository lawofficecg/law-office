Attorney Profile

Caroline Golian, Esq.

Caroline Golian is a California attorney and the founder of the Law Office of Caroline Golian. She represents clients in two primary practice areas: Personal Injury and Habitability.

In Personal Injury matters, Caroline represents individuals injured because of dangerous property conditions, negligent maintenance, unsafe walkways, slip-and-fall incidents, trip-and-fall incidents, and other preventable hazards. Her work focuses on identifying what caused the injury, preserving evidence, evaluating medical treatment and damages, and pursuing recovery from responsible parties.

In Habitability matters, Caroline represents tenants living with unsafe housing conditions, including mold exposure, water intrusion, leaks, landlord failure to repair, and other conditions that affect the safety and livability of a rental property.

Caroline earned her J.D. from the University of La Verne College of Law and her B.A. in Psychology from the University of California, Los Angeles. She is admitted to practice law in California and is listed with the State Bar of California as Bar No. 356252.

Caroline approaches each case with preparation, direct communication, and a focus on the evidence needed to prove liability, damages, and recovery. Her goal is to help clients understand their options, protect their rights, and pursue meaningful results.
