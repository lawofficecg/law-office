Practice Area

Habitability Law

Living with mold, leaks, or unsafe conditions? Every tenant deserves a safe home, and California law holds landlords responsible for mold, water damage, and other hazardous conditions. We help tenants get out of dangerous situations and recover compensation for the harm they've suffered. If your landlord has ignored your complaints, we want to hear from you.

Fee structure: Mold and habitability cases are handled on a contingency-fee basis: no attorney’s fees unless there is a recovery. Case costs are addressed separately and explained before representation begins.
