Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Information You Provide to Us

When you submit our intake form, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Best time to reach you
  • Case type (habitability or personal injury)
  • Case facts and description of your situation
  • Symptoms or injuries
  • Housing-related information (e.g. written notice status, landlord response, air or mold test results)
  • Incident date and details (for personal injury matters)
  • Medical treatment information
  • Any other information you choose to submit through the intake form or by contacting us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see "Cookies and Tracking" below)
  • Analytics data, if you have consented to analytics cookies
  • Information used to detect and prevent spam or abusive form submissions

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Evaluating your inquiry to determine whether we may be able to assist you
  • Conducting conflict checks
  • Responding to and communicating with you about your inquiry
  • Operating, maintaining, and improving this website
  • Website security and preventing spam or fraudulent submissions
  • Other legal and business purposes, such as recordkeeping and complying with applicable law

No Attorney-Client Relationship

Submitting the intake form does not create an attorney-client relationship. An attorney-client relationship is only established after a conflict check has been completed and you have been formally retained in a signed written engagement agreement with the Law Office of Caroline Golian.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following categories of third-party service providers to operate this website and process intake form submissions:

  • A form submission provider, used to route intake form submissions to our office
  • A website hosting provider
  • Google Fonts, used to load typefaces on this website (see below)
  • Analytics or cookie-management tools, only if you have consented to their use
  • Other vendors as reasonably necessary to operate our website and law practice

These providers process information only as necessary to provide their services to us and are not authorized to use your information for their own marketing purposes.

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not rent or share it with third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

This website uses browser local storage to remember your cookie consent preference so you are not asked again on each visit. No tracking or advertising cookies are set without your explicit consent.

Analytics scripts and third-party tracking tools are only loaded if you click "Accept" on the cookie consent banner. If you click "Reject," no tracking scripts are loaded at all. You can change your choice at any time using the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer.

This website also loads fonts from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). When your browser requests these fonts, Google may log that request in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. No additional data from this website is shared with Google.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you submit. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, and no method of electronic storage, is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please visit our Contact page for current phone, email, and other contact information.
